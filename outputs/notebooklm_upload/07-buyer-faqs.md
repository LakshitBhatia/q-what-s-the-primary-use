# Buyer FAQs

## What is the project?
The Palatial by Hero Homes is an ultra-luxury residential project by Hero Realty in Sector 104, Gurugram, on Dwarka Expressway.

## What are the main unit sizes?
The main mentioned sizes are 2,550 sqft, 2,833 sqft, and 3,457 sqft. The 2,833 sqft format is positioned as 3 BHK + SPR and the 3,457 sqft format as 4 BHK + SPR.

## What is the clubhouse size?
The clubhouse is mentioned as 60,000 sqft with G+4 floors.

## What is the payment plan?
The brief mentions 30:30:40 and a new 30:70 plan with staged early payments and later construction-linked payments.

## What is the rate?
The mentioned rate is 19,500 psf plus GST and PLC.

## What are the main USPs?
Hero brand trust, Dwarka Expressway location, low density, palace-inspired architecture, 60,000 sqft clubhouse, wrap-around balconies, fully fitted homes, and vehicle-free surface planning.

## What should buyers verify?
Latest price sheet, RERA, inventory, PLC, GST, possession timeline, specifications, payment schedule, transfer terms, and booking terms.
