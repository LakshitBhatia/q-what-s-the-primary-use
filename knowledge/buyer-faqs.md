# Buyer FAQs

## What is The Palatial by Hero Homes?
It is an ultra-luxury residential project by Hero Realty in Sector 104, Gurugram, positioned on Dwarka Expressway with palace-inspired architecture, low-density planning, large-format apartments, and a 60,000 sqft clubhouse.

## Which unit sizes are available?
The key mentioned sizes are 2,550 sqft, 2,833 sqft, and 3,457 sqft. The 2,833 sqft unit is positioned as 3 BHK + SPR, and the 3,457 sqft unit is positioned as 4 BHK + SPR. Penthouse options are also mentioned.

## What does SPR mean?
SPR refers to service provider room, commonly used for domestic staff or support utility.

## What is the main USP?
The main USPs are the palatial clubhouse, low-density planning, location on Dwarka Expressway, palace-inspired architecture, wrap-around balconies, fully fitted apartments, and a vehicle-free surface zone.

## What is the payment plan?
The brief mentions 30:30:40 and a new 30:70 plan. In the 30:70 plan, the buyer pays 10% after 30 days, 10% after 90 days, 10% after 240 days, and later construction-linked payments.

## What is the mentioned rate?
The mentioned rate is 19,500 psf plus GST and PLC.

## Is this suitable for investors?
It can be positioned for investors who want a branded luxury asset on Dwarka Expressway, especially if they believe in long-term corridor appreciation. The conversation should include holding period, payment plan, and resale comparisons.

## Is this suitable for end-use families?
Yes. The family-use pitch should focus on larger apartments, low density, open green areas, vehicle-free surface, school access, clubhouse lifestyle, and Hero brand trust.

## How safe is the environment for children?
The sales narrative can highlight the vehicle-free surface zone, multi-tier digital security, and proximity to Delhi Public School, Sector 104. Travel-time claims should be treated as indicative.

## What smart-home features are included?
The extracted content describes a Panasonic smart-home ecosystem with IoT controls, smart switches, living-room curtain automation, AQI management, ERV, Nanoe X, VRF systems, smart toilets, IP video door phones, digital locks, and CCTV/video surveillance. Final specifications should be verified from official documents before booking.

## Why is the 30:70 plan useful for investors?
The investor angle is capital leverage. After the initial 30%, the next major payment is described as tentatively after 4 years at super-structure completion, which may allow time for corridor appreciation before larger milestone payments. Buyers should verify exact terms.

## Is there trophy inventory?
Yes, the extracted content mentions penthouse formats around 7,000 sqft and up to 13,500 sqft, with double-height living areas and private decks mentioned in the NotebookLM extraction. Verify current availability.

## What should buyers verify before booking?
Buyers should verify RERA details, latest price sheet, inventory availability, PLC, GST, possession timeline, final specifications, layout, allotment terms, and payment-plan conditions directly from authorized sales documentation.
