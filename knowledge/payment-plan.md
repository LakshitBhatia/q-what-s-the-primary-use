# Payment Plan

## Standard Plan Mentioned
30:30:40.

## New Payment Plan Mentioned
30:70.

## 30:70 Breakdown
- After 30 days: 10%.
- After 90 days: 10%.
- After 240 days: 10%.
- Next payment tentatively after 4 years.
- At completion of super structure: 30%.
- At completion of internal plaster: 10%.
- Application of OC: 15%.
- Receipt of OC: 10%.
- Possession: 5%.

## Rate Mentioned
- Rate: 19,500 psf + GST + PLC.
- First transfer free.
- Fully loaded apartments.
- Sizes: 2,550 sqft, 2,833 sqft, 3,457 sqft.
- Offer validity mentioned: just 50 units.

## How To Explain To Buyers
For payment-sensitive buyers, avoid starting with total ticket size. First explain that the plan allows the buyer to enter with staggered early payments, then later payments become construction/milestone linked.

Recommended line:
"The key benefit is not just the 30:70 label; it is the breathing room after the initial 30%, with later payments tied to construction milestones."
