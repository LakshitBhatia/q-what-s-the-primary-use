# The Palatial by Hero Homes - Project Summary

## Positioning
The Palatial by Hero Homes is an ultra-luxury residential project in Sector 104, Gurugram, positioned on Dwarka Expressway. The pitch should focus on palace-inspired architecture, low-density living, strong connectivity, and a lifestyle-led clubhouse.

## Core Facts
- Developer: Hero Realty Pvt. Ltd.
- Location: Sector 104, Gurugram, Dwarka Expressway.
- Land area: 11+ acres.
- Towers: 5 standalone towers.
- Tower height: G+41 / G+42 / G+43.
- Density: Approximately 688 families.
- Apartments per floor: 4.
- Surface planning: Vehicle-free surface zone / no vehicle movement on outside land parcel.
- Green and open area: Around 70%.
- Basements: 3.
- Lifts: 5 including service lift.
- Floor-to-ceiling height: 3.3 m.
- Apartment state: Fully fitted apartments.
- Architecture theme: Palace-inspired / European theme.
- Smart-home ecosystem: Panasonic IoT automation, AQI management, ERV, Nanoe X, VRF systems, video door phones, digital locks, CCTV/video surveillance, and smart electrical infrastructure as described in the NotebookLM extraction.

## Hero Realty Credibility Points
- Hero Realty is the real estate arm of Hero Enterprise.
- Hero Enterprise legacy includes Hero Cycles and Hero MotoCorp.
- Hero Realty claims 5700+ happy customers, 6.02 million sqft delivered, 5.2 million sqft under development, and 230 acres of industrial parks.
- Corporate messaging emphasizes trust, sustainability, community, fitness, and long-term promise keeping.
